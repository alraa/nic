Closed joint-stock company «Scientific-investment center «Cable Technologies» is a part of the Russian group of companies, established in 1993.

Scientific-investment center «Cable Technologies» works at development and introduction of prospective wires and cables, electrical insulating and structural materials, takes part in modernization of the Russian railway system rolling-stock.

In process of our scientific-investment activity we:

  • analyze the existing market products concerning their conformity to the consumers’ demands,
  • form the technical task for the developing of new upgraded products,
  • conclude treaties with developers,
  • choose an appropriate manufacturer according to its technological and productive capacities, transportation and production costs.

  • Developing and introduction of new wires and cables isolated with thermoplastic elastomer for railroad rolling-stock.
